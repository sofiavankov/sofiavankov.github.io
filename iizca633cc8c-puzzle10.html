Once upon a time a student went to Il Caffe and left with a beer, a potential hookup, and a drunk friend. On his way home, the student came to a busy bike intersection. But when crossing the road, the student could only take himself and one other person/thing: the beer, the friend, or the hookup. If left unattended together, the friend would drink the beer, or the hookup would kiss the friend! The student's challenge was to get them all across the road without any problems. How many times did he have to cross the road?
