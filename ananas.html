ANANAS

Arte Ananas is a Contemporary Movement that aims to fuse Mexico’s art, society and agriculture with nature. For this rebranding, we chose a sophisticated, yet bold proposal in which color refers you to the richness of Mexico in a non obvious way.

Type of work: Branding, Art direction & Photography
Realization: 2020
